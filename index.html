About Me

I am a QA Tester with hands-on experience in both manual and automation testing for web and mobile applications. My background as a Mechanical Engineer and former HVAC Design Engineer gave me a strong foundation in analytical thinking, attention to detail, and end-to-end project delivery.

In recent years, I have transitioned my engineering mindset into the world of software quality assurance, where I design and execute test cases, build maintainable automation frameworks, and validate APIs and databases. I specialize in Selenium, TestNG, Postman, SQL, and CI/CD pipelines with Jenkins.

My projects include automation of an e-commerce application (login, search, checkout workflows), a music streaming web app (playlist management, user authentication, framework design), and API validation (REST endpoints, JSON/XML schemas). Each project has strengthened my ability to deliver reliable test coverage, reusable frameworks, and actionable test reports.

I am passionate about quality, collaboration, and continuous improvement, and I am eager to bring my skills in functional, regression, and automation testing into a professional QA role.
